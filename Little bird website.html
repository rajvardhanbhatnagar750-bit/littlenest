Every Child Deserves a Nest

About Us

Little Nest is a safe, loving space for orphaned and abandoned children aged 0–6. We provide shelter, early education, emotional care, and community support. Our goal is to nurture each child in a warm, caring environment where they can thrive. We believe that every child deserves not just survival, but joy, learning, and a sense of belonging.

About the Founder: Little Nest was founded by Rajvardhan Bhatnagar, a Class 10 student at Teoler Unbounded, driven by a strong sense of empathy and social responsibility. Rajvardhan envisioned a place where every child could feel safe, valued, and loved.

Our Projects

Project Sunrise

A nurturing early childhood shelter and learning center. Project Sunrise focuses on emotional, social, and intellectual growth through guided play, health check-ups, and early literacy programs.

Sunrise Stationery Drive

We collect learning materials and books to provide children with basic tools of education. Donors can contribute new or gently used items to be distributed at our shelters.

Nest Wellness Camps
